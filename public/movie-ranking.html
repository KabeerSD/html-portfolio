The Best Movies According to Angela

My Top 3 Movies of all-time

The Wolf of Wall Street

The Wolf of Wall Street is a compelling film that brilliantly showcases the excesses and debauchery of Wall Street in the 1990s. Directed by Martin Scorsese and featuring great actors, the movie combines sharp wit, dark humour and an unflinching portrayalof moral decay. Its fast paced narrative, coupled with outstanding supporting roles makes for an engaging and provocative viewing experience. The film's ability to balance its outragous moments with insightful social commentary on greed and corruption is truly what makes it stand out.

Shutter Island

"Shutter Island," directed by Martin Scorsese, is a gripping psychological thriller that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats. Leonardo DiCaprio delivers a riveting performance as a U.S. Marshal investigating a disappearance at a mysterious psychiatric facility. The film masterfully blends suspense, eerie atmospheres, and unexpected twists, creating a haunting and thought-provoking experience. With its intricate plot and compelling visuals, "Shutter Island" is a standout in the thriller genre.

Frozen

"Frozen" is a delightful animated film that captures the hearts of both children and adults. With its enchanting storyline, memorable characters, and catchy songs, it tells the tale of two sisters, Elsa and Anna, navigating the challenges of love, fear, and family. The film's stunning animation and powerful anthem "Let It Go" make it a visual and auditory feast. "Frozen" stands out for its strong messages about self-acceptance and the enduring bond of sisterhood.
